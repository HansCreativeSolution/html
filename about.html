About Adriya

AMENITIES

Adriya Pik Offers Pools, Wellness, Leisure, Playgrounds, And Social Spaces.

Prime Location

Situated At The Heart Of Pantai Indah Kapuk’s Extensive Infrastructure, ADRIYA’s Community Enjoys Accessibility To Integrated Shopping Avenues, Business Districts, Two Daily Markets, Renowned Hospitals And An Idyllic Golf Course.

Design & Layout

The Adriya Pik High-Rise Towers Feature A Modern, Elegant Design With Distinct Zones For Functionality And Luxury. The Low Zone (Floors 3–18) And High Zone (Floors 19–33) Offer Versatile Spaces, While The Refuge Floor Ensures Safety. A Serene Skygarden Crowns The Rooftop, And The Ground Floor Hosts The Lobby And Multifunctional Areas, Supported By Basement Parking. With A Sleek Glass Facade And Eco-Friendly Touches Like Rooftop Greenery, Adriya Pik Blends Modern Aesthetics With Urban Sophistication.

Navigation

Located At Jl. Pantai Indah Kapuk Boulevard, North Jakarta, ADR Tower Is Surrounded By Key Business And Leisure Destinations.
